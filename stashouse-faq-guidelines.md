## The Offer

**StashHouse Gallery Memberships** are hand-original commission artwork created by Malcolm Xavior Seven. You join by commissioning a piece in one of three sizes: Small, Medium, or Large Tilt.

Each membership includes:
- Hand-original, one-of-a-kind artwork (acrylic, oil, gold leaf on canvas)
- Certificate of authenticity
- Signature by Malcolm Xavior Seven
- Membership to the StashHouse Gallery community

**Pricing:**
- Small Tilt (8×10): $100 total | $50 down, $50 on delivery
- Medium Tilt (18×24): $200 total | $100 down, $100 on delivery
- Large Tilt (24×36): $500 total | $250 down, $250 on delivery

**First Round Limited:** 50 members total

---

## What We DON'T Create

### 🚫 No Portraits
We do not create realistic depictions of people's faces or likenesses. This is a hard rule.

This means:
- No photos of real people turned into paintings
- No realistic facial portraits of celebrities or public figures
- No photorealistic people

**Why?** We focus on the character, the emotion, the concept—not replicating faces. Characters, anime, stylized figures, and symbolic representations of people are all fair game.

## The Process

### 1. **Choose & Pay Down Payment**
   - Select your tier (Small, Medium, or Large Tilt)
   - Pay the down payment via our secure link
   - You'll receive a confirmation email

### 2. **Intake Form**
   - After payment, fill out the Commission Intake Form
   - Tell us your vision: subject, style, mood, references, inspiration
   - Be as detailed or as open as you want—we'll work with you either way

### 3. **Concept Confirmation**
   - We'll review your intake within 48 hours
   - If we need clarification, we'll reach out
   - Once approved, the 4–6 week timeline begins

### 4. **Creation**
   - Malcolm Xavior Seven creates your hand-original artwork
   - 4–6 weeks from approval to finished piece
   - We'll send a progress update if the piece requires it

### 5. **Delivery**
   - You pay the remaining balance
   - We ship your artwork with certificate of authenticity
   - You're now a StashHouse Gallery member

## Questions About Your Vision

We encourage specificity. The more you tell us, the closer we get to your exact vision.

**Share with us:**
- The subject or concept
- The style/aesthetic (realistic, abstract, geometric, etc.)
- The mood/energy (bold, serene, chaotic, etc.)
- Colors you love or want to avoid
- Cultural or personal meaning
- References and inspiration (links, images, artists)
- Any restrictions or special requests

**You can also be open:**
- "I love your work—surprise me"
- "Something bold and powerful featuring [concept]"
- "In the style of [artist/reference]"

We're experienced at translating both detailed briefs and open-ended vision into finished artwork.

## Modifications & Revisions

**During the intake phase:** We can adjust the concept based on your feedback before creation begins.

**During creation:** Major direction changes require timeline adjustments. We'll discuss with you if needed.

**After completion:** The finished artwork is final. We create pieces to stand as complete works.

## Ownership & Rights

When you purchase a membership and commission artwork:
- **You own the physical artwork**—it's yours to display, keep, sell, or gift
- **We retain the right to photograph and document the work for our portfolio and social media**
- **Commercial licensing:** If you want exclusive rights or commercial use, contact us to discuss custom terms

---

## What If Something Goes Wrong?

**If you're unhappy with the direction:** Contact us during the intake/approval phase. We want to get it right before creation starts.

**If there's a shipping issue:** We'll work with you to resolve it.

**If you need to cancel:** Down payments are non-refundable once creation has begun. If you cancel before concept approval, we can discuss options.
